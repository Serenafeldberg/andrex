# International Programs

## Incoming

Approximately 100+ exchange students annually study at San Andrés for a semester or year for an unforgettable academic and cultural experience. These students make an important contribution to the internationalization of our university.
## Outgoing

Fomentamos la movilidad estudiantil entre la Universidad de San Andrés y universidades extranjeras. Brindamos oportunidades para que puedas enriquecer tu experiencia académica, conocer otro país y apreciar la diversidad.
